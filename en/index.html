What is APIKS?

A pioneering study on the
global academic profession

The Academic Profession in the Knowledge-Based Society (APIKS) is a groundbreaking international study that explores the academic profession in more than 30 countries. Through a common survey framework, it fosters a global research community and contributes to the advancement of knowledge and professional development.

The APIKS dataset, generated in 2018-2019, includes detailed records from 20 countries, covering key areas such as academic careers, employment conditions, teaching, research, external activities, governance, and personal characteristics.

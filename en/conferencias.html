Scientific Events

International Conferences

Bilateral and global meetings organized by the APIKS consortium to foster scholarly debate on the academic profession.

June 2024

APIKS International Conference — Ljubljana, Slovenia

The gathering focused on "Early Career Researchers and Internationalization in a Changing Environment." Chilean researchers from APIKS Chile actively participated, sharing findings on early-career challenges in the Global South.

Past Event
January 2023

APIKS International Conference — Santiago, Chile

An institutional milestone: for the first time, a South American country hosted the consortium's main international conference. The event was jointly organized by PUC, Universidad de Chile, and Universidad de La Frontera.

More than 40 researchers from 20 countries participated in a hybrid format to debate post-pandemic challenges in institutional governance and academic work.

Highlighted Milestone
November 2022

Research Seminar — Kyoto, Japan

Technical and methodological seminar hosted by Kyoto University. Presentations centered on the first global comparisons using the dataset of 20 countries, emphasizing productivity differences between Asia and the West.

Past Event
June 2021

APIKS Annual Meeting — Helsinki, Finland

Held in a fully virtual format due to COVID-19 safety protocols. Discourse focused on the immediate impact of lockdowns on university teaching and emerging divides stemming from remote academic work.

Past Event
2025

Upcoming APIKS Conference — Taipei, Taiwan

The next global meeting scheduled to debate the impact of artificial intelligence technologies on undergraduate education and ethics in academic publishing.

Research Team

Faculty and researchers from leading national higher education institutions dedicated to studying the Chilean academic profession.

Pamela Guzmán, Ph.D

Country Team Leader

Universidad de La Frontera

Specialist in university governance, public policy, and the engagement of higher education with regional development. She leads the national team of APIKS Chile.

Contact ORCID

Daniela Véliz, Ph.D

Associate Researcher

Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile

Associate Professor in the Faculty of Education at PUC. Her research focuses on scientific capacity development, academic career pathways, gender equity, and international scientific collaboration.

Contact ORCID

Paulina Berríos, Ph.D

Associate Researcher

Universidad de Chile

Director of Institutional Research and Data Analysis at Universidad de Chile. Her work addresses institutional management, educational public policy, finance, and quality assurance in higher education.

Contact ORCID

Andrés Bernasconi, Ph.D

Associate Researcher

Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile

Lawyer and sociologist, Full Professor at PUC, and Director of CEPPE. An expert in educational policy, legislation, governance, and the sociology of academic organizations.

Contact ORCID

Sergio Celis, Ph.D

Associate Researcher

Universidad de Chile

Associate Professor at the Faculty of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Universidad de Chile. He researches engineering and science education, the sociology of scientific disciplines, internationalization, and knowledge transfer.

Contact ORCID

Johana Fuentes

Associate Researcher

Universidad Arturo Prat

Academic and faculty member at Universidad Arturo Prat in Iquique. She develops research lines associated with professional teacher development and inclusive pedagogical practices in geographically remote regions.

Contact ORCID

Carmen Araneda G.

Associate Researcher

Universidad de Tarapacá

Associate Professor at Universidad de Tarapacá in Arica. Her research explores the design and impact of higher education policies on territorial equity and quality management in border regions.

Contact ORCID

Mario Alarcón, Ph.D

Associate Researcher

Universidad Diego Portales

Assistant Professor in the Faculty of Social Sciences and History at UDP. His research interests encompass the sociology of professions, labor transformations, and the working conditions of academics in the knowledge society.

Contact ORCID

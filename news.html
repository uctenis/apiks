New Publication
March 15, 2024

New Article in the Prestigious Oxford Research Encyclopedia of Education

Researchers Daniela Véliz, Paulina Berríos, Pamela Guzmán, Andrés Bernasconi, and Sergio Celis publish a comprehensive study on internationalization across Chilean universities. This contribution addresses comparative public policies and institutional practices that shape the global integration of our higher education system.

The article represents one of the most robust Chilean contributions to the Oxford global reference collection in education in recent years.
